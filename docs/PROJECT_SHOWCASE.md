### Phase 1: Business Question
**Problem:** MyGym faced 18% member churn and inconsistent revenue growth.
**Question:** "How can we improve member retention while maximizing revenue per member?"

### Phase 2: Data Analysis 
- **Tools Used:** Power BI, DAX, SQL, Excel
- **Key Metrics Tracked:** 
  - Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
  - Churn Probability Score
  - Engagement Index
  - Revenue Contribution by Segment
- **Technical Challenges Solved:**
  - DAX context transition for accurate member counts
  - Data model optimization for 100K+ records
  - Real-time dashboard performance tuning

### Phase 3: Insights Generation (Medium Article)
**Article Highlights:**
1. **The 80/20 Rule Applied:** 20% of members (Elite/Premium) generate 60% of revenue
2. **Churn Prediction Model:** Identified members at risk 30 days before leaving
3. **Seasonal Patterns:** Winter signups have 40% higher retention than summer
4. **Geographic Strategy:** Sacramento outperforms other cities by 35% in member engagement

### Phase 4: Business Impact
**Quantified Results:**
- Churn reduction: 18% ‚Üí 10% (6 months post-implementation)
- Revenue per member increase: 22%
- Member satisfaction score: +15 points
- Operational cost savings: 8% through optimized staffing
